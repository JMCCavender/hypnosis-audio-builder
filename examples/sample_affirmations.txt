# Sample Affirmations for Self-Hypnosis
# Each line is a separate affirmation
#
# Writing Rules:
# - Present tense ("I am" not "I will be")
# - Sensory and descriptive language
# - Emotional words (confident, powerful, calm, magnetic)
# - No negations (avoid "not", "don't", "never")
# - Absolute statements (no "maybe", "hope", "try")

I am calm and deeply relaxed.
My mind is clear, focused, and sharp.
I breathe deeply and easily with each breath.
Peace flows through every cell of my body.
I feel confident and capable in all situations.
Every cell in my body radiates vibrant health.
I trust myself completely and absolutely.
I am worthy of success in all its forms.
My thoughts are positive, powerful, and empowering.
I release all tension with each exhale.
I am grounded, centered, and present.
Abundance flows to me naturally and effortlessly.
I feel deep gratitude for this moment.
My energy is strong, vibrant, and magnetic.
I create my reality with my thoughts and beliefs.
I am at peace with myself and the world.
Love surrounds me and flows through me always.
I embrace positive change with open arms.
My potential is unlimited and expanding.
I am exactly where I am meant to be.
I feel warmth and confidence radiating from within.
Success comes to me easily and naturally.
I attract positive people and opportunities.
My subconscious mind absorbs these truths completely.
